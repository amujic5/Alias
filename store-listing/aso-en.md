## Full description (≤4000 chars)
Alias Words is the fun, fast party word game for friends and family! Split into teams, explain the word on your screen without saying it, and race the clock to guess as many as you can. Easy to learn, impossible to put down.

🎉 THE PARTY GAME FOR EVERY GROUP
Perfect for game nights, parties, road trips, classrooms and family gatherings. From 2 players to a big group — just pass the phone and take turns. No internet needed.

🧠 EXPLAIN & GUESS
One player describes the secret word, the team guesses before time runs out. Score a point for every correct word, skip the tricky ones. It's charades with words — like Alias and Taboo, but in your pocket.

🤖 NEW: PLAY WITH AI
No one around? Challenge the AI! Explain words and watch the AI guess them, or let the AI give you clues and you guess. You can even send an AI Challenge to a friend and see who scores higher.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 FUN FOR ALL AGES
A family-friendly word game with an easy Junior deck for kids and trickier decks for adults. A great way to build vocabulary and language skills while everyone laughs together.
